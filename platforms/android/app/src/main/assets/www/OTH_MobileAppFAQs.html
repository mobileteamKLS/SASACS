FAQs for GMR Hyderabad Air Cargo App
What activities can be performed by stakeholders on GMR Hyderabad Air Cargo Mobile App?
Following activities could be performed by Freight Forwarder and Customs Broker (CB) user types on GMR Hyderabad Air Cargo Mobile App:
a. Export and Import AWB Tracking
b. Vehicle Movement Tracking
c. EGM Details
d. IGM Details
e. View Applicable Charges

How can vehicle movement be tracked for Exports and Imports?

Step 1: Login as FF/CB
Step 2: Select Exports
Step 3: Enter SB No.
Step 4: Enter Date

Screen with following tracking details will appear
a. EVT Number and Vehicle No
b. Parking area
c. Dock-in
d. Dock-out

All the VTs for that particular Shipping Bill will be displayed on the screen. The user can click on the VT that he wishes to check the status for and the drop down will showcase the status for that Particular Vehicle.
How to check the CTO PD account balance?
Step 1: Login as FF/CB
Step 2: Select Others
Step 3: Go to PD Account Balance
Step 4: Select from Dropdown - Balance for CTO and the balance will be displayed
How to check the ACS PD account balance?
Step 1: Login as FF/CB
Step 2: Select Others
Step 3: Go to PD Account Balance
Step 4: Select from Dropdown - GMR Hyderabad Air Cargo
Step 5: Select from GMR Hyderabad Air Cargo dropdown: GMR or ACS depending on your requirement and the balance will be displayed
