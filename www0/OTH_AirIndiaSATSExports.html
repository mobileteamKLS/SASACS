Notes: Common for Export and Import

1.
Consignments of Human Remains, Coffins, Unaccompanied Baggage of the deceased and Human Eyes will be exempted from the purview of TSP and Demurrage Charges.
2.
TSP Charge applicable to Newspaper and TV reel consignments shall be at 50% of the applicable charge.
3.
Charges will be levied on the Gross Weight or Chargeable Weight whichever is higher. Where there is a misdeclaration of gross weight or chargeable weight on the AWB, the actual gross weight or the actual chargeable weight will be used for the charges whichever is higher.
4.
Demurrage:
Export Cargo - Rate as given above will be charged after 12 Hrs of offloading of Cargo in Warehouse. Demurrage will be charged per day per Kg basis.
Import Cargo - From Segregation Time plus 48 hours - No Charge.
If the clearance is done on 3rd and 4th day from Flight Segregation Time then the charges are as per Slab 1 under 2(a), (b) & (c) per Kg per day. If the clearance is done after the 4th day of the Flight Segregation Time, the demurrage will be calculated cumulatively as under:
a. Day of Flight Segregation Time - 4th day : Slab 1
b. 5th Day - 29 days - (both days inclusive) : Slab 1 & Slab 2
c. Beyond 29 days : Slab 3
5.
All billing will be rounded off to the nearest Rs. 5/- as per IATA Tact rule.
6.
Special cargo (VAL) consists of live animals and day old chicks.
7.
VAL cargo includes gold bullion, currency notes, shares, share coupons, travelers cheques, diamonds (including diamonds for industrial use) diamond jewellery, watches of silver-gold-platinum and items valued at USD 1000 per Kg and above.
